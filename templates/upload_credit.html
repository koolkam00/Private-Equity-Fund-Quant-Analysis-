Expected Format

One row per loan. Column names are flexible (we match common variations).

Required:
  • Company Name
  • Fund Name
Capital & Returns:
  • Committed, Entry Loan Amount
  • Current Invested Capital
  • Realized Proceeds, Total Value
  • Gross IRR, MOIC
Loan Economics:
  • Cash Margin / Coupon, PIK Margin
  • Floor, Closing Fee, Exit Fee
  • Loan Term, Prepayment Protection
Other:
  • Status, Sector, Location
  • Warrants, Equity Investment
  • TTM Revenue (entry / current)
